Who is Cameron?

My name is Cameron Cummings (in case you missed it.), and I am a senior year student at Boise State University. I major in GIMM (Games Interactive Mobile Media), which means I want to make cool video games.

I like to play games in my free time, my favorites are Team Fortress 2, Hell Divers 2, Hollow Knight, and Outer Wilds (all subject to change, although not likely). I also like to draw, play DND, and hang around with people who are willing to tolerate me!

My main skills when it comes to making video games would be 3D Modeling. I use Blender for most of my works needs, although I'm more than willing to learn more programs. So far I've mainly done works through my college courses, but I'm working with a start up to hopefully get more of my soon to be works out into the wild.

By the way, check out the stuff I've made.
